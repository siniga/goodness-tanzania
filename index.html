About Goodness Tours & Safari

At Goodness Tours & Safari, we are dedicated to bringing the beauty and wonder of Tanzania to life through meaningful journeys that connect people to nature and each other.

Our mission is rooted in a deep love for Tanzania’s landscapes and cultures. We seek to provide authentic experiences of travel, exploration, and adventure, guiding you through Tanzania’s awe-inspiring natural settings.

Unforgettable Safari Experiences

Embark on an adventure of a lifetime with our carefully curated selection of safari activities. From thrilling game drives to serene hot air balloon rides, discover the magic of Tanzania.

Game Drives

Experience the thrill of tracking the Big Five and witnessing the great migration across the Serengeti plains in our custom-designed safari vehicles.
